The United States Freedom of Information Act ("FOIA") is a law enacted in 1966 which establishes a "right-to-know" legal process by which requests may be made for government-held information to be received freely or at minimal cost.

Foia.fyi allows you to easily navigate the often intimidating practice of submitting a FOIA request on your name or company to a government agency for free. For privacy and security concerns, Foia.fyi does not submit requests on behalf of other entities.
